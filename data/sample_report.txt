RWE is accelerating its renewable energy investments as part of its strategy to achieve net-zero emissions by 2040. At the core of this vision lie massive Offshore Wind projects in the North Sea. However, this transition is not merely a financial investment; it also necessitates a significant restructuring of human capital. One of the greatest risks in the sector is the integration of the traditional fossil fuel workforce into the green economy. In this regard, RWE supports STEM education programs and collaborates with universities to close the 'Green Skills' gap, particularly emerging in areas such as wind turbine maintenance expertise and smart grid management.
